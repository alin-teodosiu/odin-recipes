Creamy Lentil Soup

Description

Lentil soup is a fast & simple to make dish that can be customised to you preferences.

While it can be made in any season, it's particularly hearthy for a cold winter lunch.

Ingredients

NOTE: serves 1 üë§ person
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 1 medium bell pepper
  • 1 table spoon of olive oil
  • 125 g of red lentiles
  • 300 ml of water

Steps

Cooking Duration: ‚è∞ 20-30 minutes
  1. Dice the onion, carrot and the bell peper in equally sized pieces
  2. Saute the onions in a pan with olive oil for 2-3 minutes
  3. Add the carrots and cook for another 2-3 minutes
  4. Add the bell pepper and cook for another 2-3 minutes
  5. Cover with water until everything is submerged
  6. Add the lentiles and cook until soft
  7. Add spices (salt, pepper, paprika - your choice) and blitz everything until creamy
